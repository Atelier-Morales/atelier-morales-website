It is no more than life itself

And as we climbed up and looked back on the past we
had left behind us, we realized that it was made up of
three different elements:

The passage of water that would drown our memories,

the smoke that threatened to cloud our sense of certainty,

and the gust of panic that took away our dreams

Whether a dream –or our path –

we will never be able to say…
