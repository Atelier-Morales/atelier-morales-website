Archeology N°11

Atelier Morales´s photographs are printed on canvas and then intervened with painting.

Nuclear catastrophes, the responsible countries and their dramatic impact on the whole world, are their subject matter.

The images show the sumptuous interiors of the offices of the Head of States, these being places where nuclear policy is decided.

Each office shows a view to a nuclear plant.
